About Me...

The start of my career in game design and development started when I was recruited to fill in as Interim Lead Designer for Grayabyss LLC leading a team of over ten individuals remotely to develop a mobile game project using Gamemaker Studio. At that time, I was fresh off New Jersey Institute of Technology with some project management skills that they needed to lead a small team remotely for a game app. After 6 months in development, the original Lead Designer returned where I transferred to a new team as a Concept Artist preparing weapon and enemy designs for an Unreal mobile project for over 2 years until the studio shut down.

After graduating with a M.S. Game Design and Development degree from Rochester Institute of Technology, I have found myself working as an instructor for Game-U where I work one to one with students across the USA teaching them how they can bring their game ideas into reality. Each week I would work with 15 - 20 students one to one in a 1- 3 hour course with various topics.

I'd help some develop 2D and 3D modeling using Blender and other free art tools. Some would request help learning different programming languages ranging from: HTML/CSS/Javascript, Python, C#/C++. I guided them to create game project which some we would work on for months to a year or two using tools best suited for their interest and skill levels: GDevelop, Unity, Unreal Engine 4/5, Roblox, Minecraft, Godot Engine. I have also published two curriculum documents for the company to have both instructors and students learn about Godot Engine creating simple platformer projects covering the basics.
